CHRISTMAS

Christmas is the holiday of love, lights, and cheers. Christmas is the celebration of the birth of Jesus Christ. The word “Christmas” comes from the Old English phrase Cristes maesse, meaning “mass of Christ.” Santa Claus makes an appearance at every Christmas party.

NEW YEAR

New Year is a festival of happiness and contentment, and is celebrated world wide. New Year denotes the beginning of another year, full of joy and success, 1st January is the first day of every Year and is celebrated as a “New Year, new beginnings”.
